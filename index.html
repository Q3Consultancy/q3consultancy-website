Our Services

Multiple Sectors. One trusted partner.

Business Development

We embed ourselves within your organisation and act as your dedicated sales and business development function in the Gulf. This isn't outsourced sales — it's a genuine partnership where we represent your brand, values, and vision.

Our network spans governments, airlines, hospitality groups, healthcare providers, and major corporates across the UAE, wider GCC, and Africa. We know the right people and know how to open the right doors.

Whether you are entering the market for the first time or looking to scale an existing presence, Q3 delivers a structured, results-focused business development programme tailored to your sector and ambitions.

  • ✓ Market entry strategy and competitor mapping
  • ✓ Government and corporate relationship development
  • ✓ Bid support and proposal writing
  • ✓ Pipeline management and CRM reporting
  • ✓ Contract negotiation and deal closure support

Recruitment

Finding the right people in the Gulf requires more than a job board — it requires understanding the local market, cultural fit, and the specific demands of operating in the UAE. Q3 provides executive search and talent acquisition that delivers.

We support companies looking to build their teams in the region, whether that means hiring locally sourced talent, relocating specialists from abroad, or both. Our process is thorough, discreet, and focused on long-term fit.

From C-suite appointments to specialist mid-level hires, Q3 brings the same level of care and professionalism to every search mandate.

  • ✓ Executive and senior leadership search
  • ✓ Role profiling and job description development
  • ✓ Candidate screening, assessment, and shortlisting
  • ✓ Interview coordination and offer management
  • ✓ Onboarding support and 90-day check-ins

Real Estate

Relocating a business or key employees to Dubai involves significant logistical and financial decisions. Q3 simplifies this process, providing expert real estate support so your people can focus on what matters.

We work with a trusted network of property partners across the emirate to source office space, co-working arrangements, and residential properties that match your brief, budget, and timeline.

From short-let serviced apartments for incoming executives to long-term commercial leases for growing teams, Q3 ensures your real estate needs are handled with the same professionalism as every other aspect of your Gulf operations.

  • ✓ Commercial office and co-working space sourcing
  • ✓ Residential relocation for executives and families
  • ✓ Lease negotiation and landlord liaison
  • ✓ Area orientation and community introduction
  • ✓ Schools, healthcare, and lifestyle advisory
